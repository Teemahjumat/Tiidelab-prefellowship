How to cook Rice and Beans with Fish stew

Ingredients required:

  • 2 cups of white Rice
  • 1 cup of Beans
  • 1 medium-sized Mackerel
  • 5 pieces of Scotch Bonnet
  • 3 large-sized Tomatoes
  • 1 large-sized Onions
  • 50mls of Vegetable oil
  • Spices (ginger, garlic,curry and thyme)
  • Seasoning (2 cubes of maggi)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 litres of water

Steps to make Rice and Beans:

  1. Pour the water in the pot and boil on the cooker.
  2. Add washed dirt-free beans and cook till it is tender.
  3. Wash Rice with water, add to the Beans and stir well.
  4. After few mins, add salt to taste 🍚 , 🧂.
  5. Allow water to dry up and ensure rice is tender before turning off the cooker to set meal aside 🍲.
